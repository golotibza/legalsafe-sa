About Us

LegalSafe SA is a forward-thinking legal tech initiative founded by a passionate legal mind and aspiring software developer, Tebatso Gololo. We believe in the power of technology to strengthen justice, not replace it.

Our mission is to support South African legal practitioners by providing tools that:

  • Ensure compliance with legal and ethical standards;
  • Track important deadlines to prevent prescription of matters;
  • Secure and organize client documents safely in the cloud.

Why LegalSafe SA?

As a Candidate Legal Practitioner completing his journey to full admission, our founder saw the need for smart, accessible systems tailored for the South African legal profession, especially solo lawyers and small firms.

LegalSafe SA was born out of a desire to protect the profession, empower lawyers, and safeguard clients' rights...one line of code at a time.
